2D City

This 2D city simulation, developed using OpenGL with C, presents a vibrant urban landscape teeming with dynamic elements. The scene features intricately designed buildings, detailed streets, and moving cars, all rendered with smooth animations to create an engaging visual experience. The cityscape showcases a variety of architectural styles and layouts, providing a realistic representation of a bustling metropolis.

Functionalities

  • Building Models
  • Moving Cars
  • Moving Clouds
