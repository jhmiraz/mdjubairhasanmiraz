LearnHub

LearnHub is an innovative and interactive educational platform designed to facilitate learning across a wide array of subjects. It serves as a comprehensive resource for students, educators, and self-learners by providing access to popular and high-quality educational videos, articles, and quizzes. The primary goal of LearnHub is to create an engaging and effective learning environment where users can track their progress and enhance their knowledge in various subjects.

Functionalities

  • Subject Selection
  • User Authentication
  • User Profile Management
  • Progress Tracking
  • Social Features
