Landing Page

This landing page is for "JH Merch," an online store featuring a variety of t-shirts priced at 250 tk each. The page includes a prominent header with navigation links to Features, Help, and Products. The logo at the top left is clickable and returns to the homepage. The main section showcases t-shirts with a "Select" button beneath each item, allowing customers to choose their preferred products. Below the t-shirts, there's a section for customer feedback, inviting visitors to share their thoughts with a text input and a submit button.
